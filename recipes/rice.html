Rice with Tuna

Description

This is another easy yet delicious dish if you don't want to spend an hour making something

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Canned tuna
  • Soy sauce
  • Olive Olive
  • Sriracha
  • Desired spices

Steps

  1. Boil some rice
  2. Add some olive oil into it
  3. Add the tuna
  4. Add some spice if you want (I usually use grill and kari)
  5. Add Sriracha and Soy sauce
  6. Mix that shit together
  7. Enjoy
