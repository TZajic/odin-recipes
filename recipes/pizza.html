Toast Pizza

Description

This is a dish I found out about while in college, it's quick, delicious and cheap.

Ingredients

  • Toast bread
  • Salami (whichever you prefer, I tend to use Poliƒçan)
  • Ketchup
  • Cheese (slices or shredded)
  • Pizza spice

Steps

  1. Put preferable amount of the bread on a baking tray
  2. Add ketchup on each piece and spread it
  3. Sprinkle it with some spice
  4. Put desirable amount of salami on in (for me, it's usually three slices
  5. Cover it with cheese
  6. Bake until it seems done
  7. Enjoy
