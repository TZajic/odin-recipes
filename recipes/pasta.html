Pasta with Ketchup

Description

This is a traditional dish for many children in our country. Mine is specific in a way that I add Soy sauce to it to spice the flavour up a bit.

Ingredients

  • Pasta of your own liking
  • Ketchup
  • Soy sauce
  • butter

Steps

  1. Boil the chosen pasta until aldente (or to your preference)
  2. Put the pasta into a bowl
  3. Add butter and stir until it is all buttery
  4. Add ketchup and Soy sauce in whichever amount you prefer
  5. Enjoy
